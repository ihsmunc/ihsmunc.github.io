The IHSMUN Club Conference

The IHSMUN Club Conference on its third run is
bigger and better than ever. With an award-winning
Secretariat and Executive Board, this year's
IHSMUNC is set for the highest level of discussion.
Two days of breathtaking debate will be sure to
leave you wanting for more, so get your suits ready,
and your binders out for IHSMUNC!

Dear Students, International Politics Enthusiasts and Club members,

It gives me great pleasure and pride in welcoming you all to the experience of your lifetime- IHSMUNC. Today politics is a dirty game and along with climate change and terrorism poses a great threat to the international community. Therefore a redefining of principles is required. This message along with the concepts of security and sustainability make up the theme for this year’s conference. IHSMUN is either going to be the crowning glory of your MUN career or the first step towards your MUN Adventure.

In addition, we expect to see truly diplomatic debates and also to reach real life solutions to global issues. It promises to be an intense, pressure packed environment that would most definitely put to test the composure as well as impulsiveness of the Committee’s delegates and Chairs. Our organizing team is working very hard to create an incredible experience for all of you and we cannot wait to show you what IHSMUN has in store, as we hope it will exceed all your expectations. We couldn´t be more excited to host you in December and we look forward to meeting each one of you at IHSMUNC!

Yash Bhatia

Secretary General of IHSMUNC'18
